What is

Greamer is an internet based Crowdfunding Platform specific for the development of great games including video game, PC game, independent game etc. It will be the first game Crowdfunding Platform all over China Mainland and Hong Kong. There are three main functions of the online platform: promotion, public funding and communication. The operation of the platform can be summarized that developer promote their projects on the platform by building their own project page on our platform and people(mainly players) can choose to fund the games in which they are interested. The communication services will be provided to both projects developer and platform visitors to create great communication environment and to enhance our mission: bring good games into reality and promote the culture of great games!

The name Greamer is created by combining “Game” and “Dreamer”. It represents our mission to bring good games into reality and promote the culture of great games. We hope to bring the dream comes true.
